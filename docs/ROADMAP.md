# Caudillo Bay - Development Roadmap

## Phase 1: Stability & Polish (Month 1-2)
- **Patch 1.1**: UI scaling for Ultrawide (21:9) and High-DPI displays.
- **Patch 1.2**: Custom keybindings and localization improvements (Spanish/Portuguese).
- **Quality of Life**: Improved feedback for pollution and tourism modifiers.

## Phase 2: Content Expansion (Month 3-4)
- **Free Update: "The Secret Police"**: New ministry building, covert operations on the island, and a new Nationalist leader.
- **DLC 1: "Criminal Syndicate"**: Introduce organized crime mechanics, smuggling docks, and a new Bodyguard: "El Capo".

## Phase 3: Industrial Revolution (Month 5-6)
- **Free Update: "Modernization"**: New Tier 4 buildings (Electronics, Pharmaceuticals).
- **DLC 2: "Tourism Paradise"**: Luxury mega-resorts, private islands, and environmentalist faction overhaul.

## Long-term Strategy
- **Architecture Migration**: Transitioning high-agent count systems (Citizens/Traffic) to **Unity ECS/DOTS** to support 50k+ population.
- **Console Porting**: Optimization for Mac, Xbox, and PlayStation.
