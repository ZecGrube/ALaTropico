# Caudillo Bay - Game Design Document (GDD)

## 1. Concept Overview
**Working Title:** Caudillo Bay
**Elevated Pitch:** A Caribbean city-builder where the economy is the engine of your political survival. You are El Presidente, tasked with building a thriving island nation while outmaneuvering internal factions and global superpowers.

## 2. Genre & Pillars
**Genre:** Political-Economic Simulator / City-Builder
**Subgenre:** Satirical Sandbox with Spy-Thriller elements.

### 2.1 Core Pillars
1. **Infrastructure Drives Politics:** Every building choice affects your relationship with the factions (e.g., a luxury hotel pleases Capitalists but may anger Communists).
2. **Dynamic Economy:** A complex resource-driven economy with deep production chains (Sugar -> Rum -> Luxury Rum).
3. **Political Survival:** Manage 5 internal factions (Capitalists, Communists, Nationalists, Religious, Environmentalists) to stay in power for 50 years.
4. **Global Influence:** A separate strategic layer on the Global Map where you interact with the USA and USSR via agents and trade.

## 3. Stylistics & Atmosphere
### 3.1 Visual Style
- **Tropical Satire:** Bright, lush Caribbean colors (turquoise ocean, white sand, deep green palms) juxtaposed with brutalist apartment blocks and gold-plated presidential statues.
- **Kitsch Elements:** Neon signs, propaganda posters, and extravagant architectural choices for El Presidente.

### 3.2 Audio & Music
- **Rhythmic Beats:** Salsa, Mambo, and Bolero for the background track.
- **Narrative Radio:** A talk-radio station that reports on the "magnificent wisdom" of El Presidente and the latest "counter-revolutionary" activities.

### 3.3 User Interface (UI)
- **Diegetic Design:** Menus styled as wooden tablets with gold lettering, nautical maps, and typewritten bureaucratic decrees.
- **Iconography:** Distinct, colorful icons for resources and buildings that fit the 1950s-1980s aesthetic.

## 4. Key Mechanics (Brief)
- **Procedural Island Generation:** Each session offers new terrain challenges.
- **Building Grid:** Drag-and-drop construction of 80+ building types.
- **Resource Logistics:** Automatic and player-directed transport of 50+ resource types.
- **ECS-Based Agents:** Thousands of citizens simulated with individual loyalty, wealth, and home/job associations.
- **The Decree System:** Direct interventions into national life that cost "Mandate" points and alter faction loyalty.

## 5. Eras of Progression
1. **Colonial Legacy:** Basic survival and agriculture.
2. **Industrialization:** Factories, electricity, and early tourism.
3. **Modern State:** High-value exports, international airports, and espionage.
4. **High-Tech & Globalization:** Electronics, aerospace, and nuclear power.
