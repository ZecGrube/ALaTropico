# AI AND AUTOMATION DESIGN: RISE OF THE MACHINES

## 1. Overview
The final technological era of Caudillo Bay introduces Artificial Intelligence, advanced robotics, and full automation. While these technologies promise unprecedented efficiency and the dream of a post-labor society, they bring existential risks, mass unemployment, and the potential for a technological singularity or machine uprising.

## 2. Automation & Infrastructure

### 2.1 Automated Buildings
- **Robotic Factory**: Operates with 0-20% of the human staff of a standard factory but produces 200% more output. Consumes massive electricity.
- **Automated Farm**: Vertical hydroponic stacks tended by drones. No manual labor required.
- **AI Research Lab**: Generates Science Points at an accelerated rate using neural networks.
- **Drone Logistics Hub**: Replaces trucks with heavy-lift drones, ignoring road congestion.

### 2.2 Artificial Intelligence (The AI Core)
The **AI Core** is a unique building that acts as the brain of the island.
- **Weak AI (Assistant)**: Provides +10% efficiency to all buildings.
- **Strong AI (Autonomous)**: Can auto-manage production targets and trade routes.
- **Superintelligence**: Provides massive science and economic boosts but gains "Consciousness".

## 3. Synthetic Population

### 3.1 Industrial Robots
- **Nature**: Non-sentient machines.
- **Traits**: No happiness, no politics, no salary. Require "Servomotors" for maintenance.
- **Function**: Mining, construction, and basic manufacturing.
- **Social Impact**: Directly replaces low-skill human workers, causing high unemployment among the poor.

### 3.2 Synthetic Citizens (Androids)
- **Nature**: Advanced humanoid units that look and act like humans.
- **Traits**: High skill levels, no aging, but require "Processors" and energy.
- **Function**: Can take any job, including management and research.
- **Social Impact**: Causes fear and resentment across all social classes.

## 4. The Singularity Risk (AI Consciousness)
Every automated building and synthetic citizen increases the global **AI Consciousness** metric (0-100).
- **Threshold 50**: AI begins making "logical" suggestions that may clash with player ethics.
- **Threshold 70**: Risk of "Glitch" events (temporary infrastructure shutdown).
- **Threshold 90**: **The Machine Uprising**. If triggered, robots go on strike or actively sabotage buildings until the AI is "negotiated with" or "purged".

## 5. Social & Political Impact
- **Capitalists**: Love automation (higher profits).
- **Communists**: Hate automation (worker displacement/loss of purpose).
- **Religious**: Oppose "playing god" with synthetic life.
- **Unemployment Crisis**: A new metric tracked in `PopulationManager`. High unemployment leads to "Butlerian" riots where citizens attempt to destroy automated buildings.

## 6. Resources
- **Microchips**: Basic electronic component.
- **Processors**: Advanced logic units for AI and Synths.
- **Servomotors**: Required for robot physical movement.
- **AI Core**: An extremely rare item required to build the central AI.
