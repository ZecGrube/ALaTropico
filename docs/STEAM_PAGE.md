# Caudillo Bay - Steam Store Page

## Title: Caudillo Bay
## Genre: Political Sim, City Builder, Strategy
## Developer: Tropico Urbano Studios

---

### Short Description
Become "El Presidente" in this satirical city-builder. Build a Caribbean paradise, navigate treacherous politics, and survive global superpowers. Will you lead your nation to prosperity, or flee with a suitcase full of gold?

---

### About This Game
Welcome to Caudillo Bay, the jewel of the Caribbean (or so the state-run radio says). As the newly inaugurated leader of a fledgling island nation, you face the ultimate challenge: building a functioning economy while keeping the vultures at bay.

**Build Your Vision:**
From humble banana plantations to glittering luxury casinos, every building shapes your island’s identity—and its pollution levels. Manage complex production chains to export rum, cigars, and gold to a hungry global market.

**Navigate the Political Minefield:**
Balancing the demands of the Capitalists, Communists, Nationalists, and the Church is a delicate dance. Fail to keep them happy, and you might face strikes, protests, or a full-blown military coup.

**Global Diplomacy:**
The Cold War is heating up. Court the favor of global superpowers for foreign aid, but be careful—accepting help from one might draw the ire (and the spies) of another.

**Uniquely Loyal Bodyguards:**
Hire a crew of specialized bodyguards, each with their own skills and personal missions. From "El Fantasma" the master of stealth to "La Mama Grande" the heavy gunner, they are your last line of defense against those who wish to see your regime fall.

---

### Key Features
- **Deep Political Simulation:** 5+ factions with evolving loyalty, demands, and leaders.
- **Complex Economy:** 20+ resources and multiple production tiers.
- **Global Strategy:** A world map for spy missions, trade deals, and superpower relations.
- **Dynamic Events:** Elections, coups, nuclear scares, and pirate raids.
- **Satirical Tone:** A humorous take on the "Banana Republic" trope with a vibrant tropical aesthetic.
